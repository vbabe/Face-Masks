WHISPER LACE COLLECTION IS COMMITED TO PROVIDING SUPPORT DURING THE COVID19 PANDEMIC

Whisper Lace Collection is a company that specializes in nightwear and intimates. Established in January 2019. All items are made in America from ethical and locally sourced fabrics. Hand sew items help to circulate revenue in communities that are historically disadvantaged and looked over. Whisper Lace is proudly BLACK OWNED. With always the people in mind, Whisper Lace began production of face mask to suit the medical needs of the community after the recent outbreak of COVID19 pandemic. Whisper Lace cares for the people and hopes recent production of face masks ensures the saftey of individuals as they move through their cities and communities.
